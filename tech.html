HTC VIVE

The HTC Vive is a virtual reality headset developed by HTC and Valve.
The headset uses "room scale" tracking technology, allowing the user to move in 3D space and use motion-tracked handheld controllers to interact with the environment.

Oculus Rift

Oculus Rift is a line of virtual reality headsets developed and manufactured by Oculus VR, a division of Facebook Inc., released on March 28, 2016.
The Oculus Rift received generally positive reviews from gaming and tech websites.
